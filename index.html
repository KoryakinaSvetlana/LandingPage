About

La Langouste restaurant is situated in the heart of Nice, near the famous Avenue Jean Medecin and Nice Etoile shopping centre. Discover a real haven of peace just a short walk from the centre agitation.

Rich colours and inimitable Mediterranean flavours are in the heart of the concept. Attentive to the smallest details, La Langouste restaurant offers a peaceful and warm atmosphere. You will be seduced by our personnalized and attentive service as well as harmonious dishes that will make your visit unforgettable.

La Langouste is a synonym of investigation of new horizons of the Mediterranean food. Whatever the choice of our guests : a business meal in a calm and comfortable atmosphere, a romantic dinner near the fireplace or a lunch in the light-bathed veranda, every visit to La Langouste assures you a unique and great moment.

Cuisine

The kitchen is the heart of the restaurant La Langouste. Its principle accent is on the harmonious combination of various flavours of the mediterranean region and its culinary traditions. Fresh and quality products, an eye-catching presentation, a dose of creativity and a lot of passion : here are the ingredients of the dishes that delight the taste buds of our guests.

The restaurant gathers a team of passionate people who put their whole heart to make the visitors live a unique experience. It is thanks to this authenticity that La Langouste has been recognised "Restaurant de Qualité" by the Collège Culinaire de France only three months after the opening.

The kitchen of La Langouste is inseparable from its atmosphere where all the gastronomic shades come together to offer you a pleasurable surprise.

Private events

Birthday, engagement, wedding, garden party, business events... La Langouste offers you to make tailor-made events according to your wishes and your budget. Privatise the garden, the veranda, the large room or all the spaces to impress your guests thanks to a meal and an atmosphere that will leave them an imperishable memory.

To make your event remarkable, La Langouste assists you by offering : music band, photographer, artist show, personnalised cake, valet, dancefloor, decoration, etc. We are receptive to all your ideas, even the most extravagant! Do not hesitate to send us your requests, we will reply in a short delay.
